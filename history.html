History

The Protolang conference series has started out as a series of smaller workshops held in Toruń, Poland, and has grown into a larger conference with around 100 participants. Below is a list of Protolang conferences that have taken place so far along with their plenary speakers.

2009 Toruń - Terrence Deacon, Jim Hurford, Katie Slocombe, Jordan Zlatev

2011 Toruń - John Gowlett, Juliane Kaminski, Adam Kendon, Katie Slocombe

2013 Wroclaw - Josep Call, Robin Dunbar, Peter Gärdenfors, Tomasz Krzeszowski

2015 Rome - Michael Corballis, Dan Dediu, Francesco d'Errico, Daniel Dor,Ian Tattersall, Elisabetta Visalberghi

2017 Barcelona - Sonja Vernes, Olga Feher, Martin Kuhlwilm, João Zilhão

2019 Lisbon - Alexandre Castro-Caldas, Catherine Hobaiter, João Zilhão, Klaus Zuberbühler, Luc Steels, Rafael Núñez, Roslyn Frank, Tania Kuteva, Tetsuro Matsuzawa

2021 Düsseldorf (online) - Michel DeGraff, Katja Liebal, Asifa Majid, Albert Newen, Cailin O'Connor, Asli Ozyurek

2023 Rome - Dean Falk, Marlen Fröhlich, Simon Kirby, Steven Mithen

2025 Vienna - Pritty Patel-Grosz, Anne Kandler, Tecumseh Fitch
